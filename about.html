ABOUT US

Rachael Ray has always loved taming to her plants. What started as a hobbie in middle school, grew into a deep love and passion. After also finding a love for business, the dream of opening her own plant nursery blossomed. In spring 2014, SunRay Plant Nursery finally opened, bringing those dreams to life.

OUR MISSION

SunRay Plant Nursery strives to provide the healthiest, thriving plants and knowledge to everyone, whether for a gift, hobby, or passion.
