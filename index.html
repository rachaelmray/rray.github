PRODUCT OF THE WEEK

Native to South Africa, Swaziland, and Zimbabwe, the String of Hearts (ceropegia woodii) is a beautiful addition to any home. The heart-shaped leaves come in a variety of colors that can change with differentiating care. The plant can grow to hang an average of three to nine feet long.

PLANT CARE

For greener leaves, keep the soil moist and leave in direct sunlight for about 6 hours a day. For pink-toned leaves, water less frequently and expose the plant to more sunlight. Water just enough to wet the top of the soil.
